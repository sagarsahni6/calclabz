Age Calculator

Calculate your exact age in years, months, days, hours, and minutes from your date of birth. Find days until your next birthday. Free age calculator — instant results.

What is Age Calculator?

The Age Calculator determines your exact age in years, months, days, hours, and minutes from your date of birth. It also shows your next birthday countdown and the day of the week you were born.

How to Calculate Exact Age

  1. Enter your date of birth (DD/MM/YYYY)
  2. Click Calculate to see your exact age in years, months, days, hours, and minutes
  3. Also see the countdown to your next birthday and your birth weekday

How Age Calculator is Calculated

Age is calculated by computing the calendar difference between today's date and your birth date. It correctly handles varying month lengths (28–31 days) and leap years (February 29).

Worked Example

Born on 15 March 1995, calculated on 9 April 2026: Age = 31 years, 0 months, 25 days. Total days lived: approximately 11,348 days.

Common Use Cases

  • Exact age verification for documents
  • Birthday countdown
  • Age eligibility checking (voting, insurance)
  • Days lived milestone tracking

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Entering the date format incorrectly — use DD/MM/YYYY (Indian format) to avoid swapping day and month.
  • Confusion between completed years and running year — this calculator shows completed years (31 years means you have fully completed 31 years).
  • Expecting exact hour/minute precision — unless you enter your exact birth time, hours and minutes are calculated from midnight.
  • Leap year birthday confusion — if born on Feb 29, most legal systems consider March 1 as the birthday in non-leap years.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is my exact age today?

Enter your date of birth in the calculator above to get your exact age in years, months, days, hours, and even minutes — calculated as of right now.

How do I calculate age in days?

Age in days = (Today's date – Birth date) in days. Our calculator shows this automatically along with age in years, months, weeks, hours, and minutes.
